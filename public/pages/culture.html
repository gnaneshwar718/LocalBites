Masala Dosa

A thick, crispy fermented crepe filled with spiced potatoes. Bengaluru's version is distinctively golden, butter-rich, and served with spicy coconut chutney and sambar.

Bisi Bele Bath

A wholesome "hot lentil rice" dish made with rice, lentils, vegetables, and a complex spice blend featuring tamarind and jaggery. It's a cornerstone of Karnataka's culinary heritage.

Nutritious finger millet balls, a staple of Old Mysore region's agricultural roots. It is traditionally served with spicy saaru or bassaru and is known for its incredible health benefits.
